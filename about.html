My Design Approach

I start with player intent: what the player needs to feel, learn, or achieve in a given moment. From there, I design mechanics and interfaces that support clear decisions and rewarding feedback.

  • Build a clear core loop and test it early with lightweight prototypes.
  • Reduce friction with intuitive UI, readable hierarchy, and purposeful motion.
  • Use sound, timing, and visual cues to improve game feel and clarity.
  • Iterate fast based on playtesting and real usage patterns.
